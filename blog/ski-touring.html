Ski Touring in the Sharr: Europe's Hidden Powder Paradise

Ski de rando dans le Sharr : le paradis caché de la poudreuse

Ski turne në Sharr: parajsa e fshehur e borës në Evropë

February 28, 2026 · 6 min read

Every winter, while skiers queue for overcrowded lifts in the Alps, the Sharr Mountains sit quietly under meters of untouched powder. No lift lines. No crowds. No groomed runs. Just pure, raw ski touring in one of Europe's last truly wild mountain ranges.

The Snow

The Sharr Mountains receive consistent snowfall from December through March. Continental influence brings cold, dry powder — the kind that makes you whoop with every turn. At higher elevations, good snow conditions can last well into April.

The Terrain

Think wide-open bowls above treeline, perfectly spaced birch forests for tree runs, and couloirs for those seeking steeper challenges. The elevation range of 1,500m to 2,700m offers incredible variety in a compact area.

Why It's Special

In a word: solitude. You can skin up for an hour and have an entire mountain face to yourself. No helicopters, no cat tracks, no other groups. Just you, your guide, and endless untracked snow.

"Three days of touring and we didn't see another skier. Fresh tracks every single run. This place is ten years behind the Alps — in the best possible way." — Andreas, Austria

Our Winter Package

Our ski touring package includes experienced mountain guides with avalanche training, all safety equipment (beacon, probe, shovel), comfortable accommodation, and warming meals after cold days on the mountain. We also offer snowshoe excursions for non-skiers.

Chaque hiver, pendant que les skieurs font la queue aux remontées bondées des Alpes, les montagnes de Sharr reposent silencieusement sous des mètres de poudreuse vierge. Aucune file. Aucune foule. Aucune piste damée.

La neige

Les montagnes de Sharr reçoivent des chutes de neige régulières de décembre à mars. L'influence continentale apporte une poudreuse froide et sèche — celle qui vous fait pousser des cris de joie à chaque virage.

Le terrain

Des vastes cuvettes au-dessus de la limite des arbres, des forêts de bouleaux parfaitement espacées pour le ski en sous-bois, et des couloirs pour ceux qui cherchent plus raide.

Pourquoi c'est spécial

En un mot : la solitude. Vous pouvez monter en peaux pendant une heure et avoir tout un versant pour vous seul.

"Trois jours de rando et nous n'avons pas vu un seul autre skieur. Des traces fraîches à chaque descente." — Andreas, Autriche

Notre forfait hiver

Notre forfait ski de randonnée comprend des guides avec formation avalanche, tout l'équipement de sécurité (DVA, sonde, pelle), hébergement et repas réchauffants.

Çdo dimër, ndërsa skiatorët presin në radhë në Alpe, Malet e Sharrit pushojnë në heshtje nën metra borë të paprekur.

Bora

Malet e Sharrit marrin rreshje të rregullta bore nga dhjetori deri në mars.
